Welcome to Somali Cash Consortium

Since the Cash Consortium began work in Jan 2018, it has assisted over 1,000,000 Somalis. It builds better and more robust cash transfer systems by working with all stakeholders to streamline each stage of the cash-transfer process, from community registrations to payment aggregation, reporting, forecasting and coordination.

ECHO HIP 2024

The Humanitarian Implementation Plan (HIP)in Somalia aims to address urgent humanitarian needs and support resilience in a context marked by ongoing conflict, natural disasters, and economic instability.It is designed to deliver timely and effective humanitarian aid while fostering local capacities to support long-term development
